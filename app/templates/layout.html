SYSTEM GUIDE: ECONOMY

XP & PROGRESSION

• The XP requirement curve is exponential. However, all rewards mathematically scale to your current level so progression speed remains static.

• Earning Level 100 will take an active player roughly 2 Years of consistency.

  • Dailies: 10% Level Cap / day
  • A-Rank Quest: 12% Level Cap
  • Void Focus: 2.5% Level Cap / hour

CURRENCIES

  • Coins (C): Used exclusively for System Shop artifacts. Earned via Void Focus (10/hr) and B+ Rank Quests. Endgame gear costs up to 15,000 C.
  • Gold (G): Earned exclusively via completing all sequence dailies (100/day). Used for real-world rewards like a "Night Out" (600G).

ATTRIBUTES (LIFE AREAS)

• Allocate points upon leveling up to permanently increase your capabilities.

  • Strength: Physical fitness, gym consistency, and diet adherence.
  • Intelligence: University work, coding, reading, and learning.
  • Agility: Speed of task completion and defeating procrastination.
  • Sense: Meditation, socializing, and networking.
  • Vitality: Sleep consistency, health, and energy preservation.

THE PENALTY SYSTEM

• Missing daily quests triggers the Penalty Zone.

• Your UI will turn red, you cannot buy from the shop, and you receive a -20% Stat Debuff.

• Clear the penalty immediately by completing a grueling Survival Quest.

RECOVERY & SLEEP

• The system monitors your physical condition.

  • Well Rested: Log 7+ hours of sleep to gain a 1.1x (+10%) XP multiplier.
  • Exhausted: Sleeping less than 6 hours breaks your streak and ruins your conditioning, applying a 0.5x (-50%) XP multiplier.
  • Use the Elixir of Life from the shop to purge exhaustion.

TITLES & SHADOWS

• Titles: Unlockable achievements (e.g., "The One Who Overcame Adversity") that provide unique XP or stat modifiers when equipped.

• The Shadow Army: Completing S-Rank quests allows you to "Arise" the challenge, granting a permanent, stacking passive buff.
