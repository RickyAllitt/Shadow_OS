SYSTEM GUIDE: ECONOMY

XP & PROGRESSION

• The XP requirement curve is exponential. However, all rewards mathematically scale to your current level so progression speed remains completely static.

• Earning Level 100 will take an active player roughly 2 Years of consistency.

  • Dailies: 10% Level Cap / day
  • A-Rank Quest: 12% Level Cap
  • Void Focus: 2.5% Level Cap / hour

CURRENCIES

  • Coins (C): Used exclusively for System Shop artifacts. Earned via Void Focus (10/hr) and B+ Rank Quests. Endgame gear costs up to 15,000 C (10+ Months of saving).
  • Gold (G): Earned exclusively via completing all sequence dailies (100/day). Used for real-world rewards like a "Night Out" (600G).

PENALTIES

• Missing dailies triggers a -20% Stat Debuff. Clear it via Survival Quests.

• Sleeping less than 6 hours breaks your Streak and ruins your conditioning (-XP). Use the Elixir of Life to rescue it.
